Journal Entry Guidelines
Double-Entry Bookkeeping:
  • Every transaction affects at least two accounts
  • Total debits must equal total credits
  • Assets and Expenses increase with debits
  • Liabilities, Equity, and Income increase with credits
Journal Entry Rules:
  • Each line must have either debit OR credit (not both)
  • At least two lines are required
  • Description should be clear and specific
  • Use appropriate source module
Common Transactions:
  • Cash Sale: Debit Cash, Credit Income
  • Purchase: Debit Expense, Credit Cash/Payable
  • Payment: Debit Payable, Credit Cash
  • Deposit: Debit Bank, Credit Cash
